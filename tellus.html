Are we a good fit?

The Deutsch Foundation welcomes innovative ideas from individuals and organizations that are aligned with the Foundation’s values and interests: say more about this.

The Foundation does not accept unsolicited proposals or requests for grants. Please do not submit any formal request directly to the Foundation. Just submit the information requested in this form via this website. Foundation staff will review all materials submitted via this website that comply with these guidelines. Please limit your response to each question with 500 words or less. Receipt of submitted materials will be acknowledged by return email sent by a human. We try to respond quickly as possible!
