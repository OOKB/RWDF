The Deutsch Foundation welcomes innovative ideas from individuals and organizations that are aligned with the Foundation’s values and interests: say more about this.

The Foundation does not accept unsolicited proposals or requests for grants. Please do not submit any formal request directly to the Foundation. Just submit the information requested in this form via this website. Foundation staff will review all materials submitted via this website that comply with these guidelines.
