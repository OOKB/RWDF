BIOFAB

Deutsch has a recurring commitment to fund the incredible work coming from the Biobab at the University of Maryland College Park. Researchers are creating microscale "living laboratories" to investigate biochemical reaction pathways, and "biological nanofactories" capable of initiating communications between bacteria in a major, multi-disciplinary research program in biofabrication.
